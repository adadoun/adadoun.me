Intro

I'm Amine Dadoun, doctoral student in Data Science. I work as a Data Scientist in Amadeus IT, where we work everyday on shaping the future of travel, and study as a PhD student in Eurecom.

My research focuses on recommendation systems applied to the world of travel and tourism. My research topics are as follows:

  • Deep Learning based Recommender Systems
  • Knowledge Graph & Word Embeddings for Recommender Systems
  • Session Based Recommender Systems
